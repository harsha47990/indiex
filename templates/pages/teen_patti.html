📖 Teen Patti — Complete Rules

🃏 What is Teen Patti?

Teen Patti (Three Cards) is a popular Indian card game similar to 3-Card Poker. Players are dealt cards and bet on who has the best hand.

🎲 How a Round Works

  1. Room Admin sets the Table Amount and starts the game
  2. Each player is dealt 3 cards (or 2 in 2-Card mode) face down
  3. Players take turns clockwise — you can play Blind (without seeing cards) or Seen (after seeing cards)
  4. Each turn you must bet to stay in, or Fold to drop out
  5. When only 2 players remain, either can call a Show to compare hands
  6. The winner takes the entire pot!

💰 Betting

  • Blind bet = Table Amount (e.g. 10 🪙)
  • Seen bet = 2× Table Amount (e.g. 20 🪙)
  • Bets go into the Pot — winner takes all
  • If you can't afford the bet, you're auto-folded

🏆 Hand Rankings (Highest → Lowest)

1 🔥 Trail (Three of a Kind) A♠ A♥ A♦ Three cards of the same rank. A-A-A is the highest.
2 ✨ Straight Flush A♠ K♠ Q♠ Three consecutive cards of the same suit.
3 📈 Straight (Sequence) A♠ K♥ Q♦ Three consecutive cards, any suits.
4 💎 Flush (Color) K♠ 9♠ 4♠ Three cards of the same suit, not in sequence.
5 👯 Pair A♠ A♥ 7♦ Two cards of the same rank. Higher pair wins.
6 🃏 High Card A♠ 9♥ 4♦ No combination. Highest card decides.

📊 Straight Order

A-K-Q (highest) → A-2-3 (2nd highest) → K-Q-J → Q-J-10 → … → 4-3-2 (lowest)

💡 A-2-3 is a special wrap and ranks just below A-K-Q.

⚖️ Tiebreakers

  • Same hand type → compare highest card, then next, then lowest
  • Exact tie → the player who initiated Show loses

🙈 Blind

Play without seeing your cards. Costs 1× Table Amount. You stay "blind" and can see your cards later.

👁️ Seen

Look at your cards. Costs 2× Table Amount per turn. Once seen, you stay seen.

🏳️ Fold

Drop out of the round. You lose all coins you've bet so far.

🃏 Show

Available when only 2 players remain. Both players reveal their cards. The better hand wins the pot.

  • Blind player pays 1× Table Amount for Show
  • Seen player pays 2× Table Amount for Show

🤝 Side Show

Request to compare cards privately with the previous player. Both must be "Seen". Unlocked after 3 rounds of betting.

  • Only works when more than 2 players remain
  • Costs 2× Table Amount
  • Loser is folded, winner continues
  • Both players see each other's cards briefly

🎯 Normal

Standard 3-card Teen Patti. Best hand wins. No special rules.

✂️ 2-Card Mode

Each player gets 2 cards instead of 3. The system imagines the best possible 3rd card for your hand.

  • Same rank → Trail (phantom makes triple)
  • Same suit + within 2 ranks → Straight Flush
  • Different suit + within 2 ranks → Straight
  • Same suit + far apart → Flush
  • Otherwise → Pair (phantom copies higher card)

💡 No High Card exists in 2-card mode — worst case is always a Pair!

💥 4-Card Mode

Each player gets 4 cards instead of 3. The system evaluates all 4 possible 3-card combinations and picks the best hand.

  • C(4,3) = 4 combinations checked automatically
  • Standard hand rankings apply (Trail → High Card)
  • More cards = stronger hands on average

💡 You see all 4 cards, but the best 3-card combo determines your hand!

🃏 Joker Mode

One card is flipped as the Joker. All cards of that rank become wild cards — they can represent any card to make your best hand.

💡 If you hold a joker card, your hand name shows "(Joker)" next to it.

🔄 Muflis Mode

Everything is reversed — the lowest hand wins! High Card beats Trail. Aim for the worst possible hand.

💡 Same rankings, just flipped. 4-3-2 offsuit is the best hand!
